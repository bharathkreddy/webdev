Klay & Clin

Where every turn of the wheel shapes not just clay, but dreams. Nestled in the heart of Uplands Road, our studio is a sanctuary for artists and enthusiasts alike, eager to explore the timeless art of pottery. Here, every piece tells a story, every curve holds a breath, and every glaze whispers secrets of the earth.

Dive into a hands-on journey through texture, form, and color as you bring your unique visions to life. Whether you're a seasoned potter or touching clay for the first time, our studio offers a space to connect, create, and celebrate the magic of ceramics.

Join us, and let the clay mold your imagination.
